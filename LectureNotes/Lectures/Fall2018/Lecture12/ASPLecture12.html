Definition 1.1.

A Levy process with drift rate α, diffusion rate σ, and jump kernel ν is a stochastic process with distribution X0=0

Fact: (Levy-Khinchine) Any Markov process on ℝ with X0=0 and stationary independent increments is Levy.

i.e. a Markov process such that

  2. 2.
