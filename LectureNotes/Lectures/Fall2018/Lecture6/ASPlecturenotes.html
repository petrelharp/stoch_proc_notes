2 Examples

Question:

Suppose we have arrival times on [0,∞). Are they (uniform) Poisson?

Let T1,T2.. be the sorted points, compare the distribution of (Tk+1-Tk)k=1N inter-arrival intervals to the exponential distribution. e.g by Kolmogorov-Smirnov test (say that 10 times fast).

We discussed two approaches to this problem in class including: looking the the length between the points or sectioning it into areas to examine the distribution. We would look at the distribution of the length between points, and there should be an equal distribution of lengths. If we section the points in our given areas and with a large enough area the points should be evenly distributed. If the points are not evenly distributed then it is not a uniform poisson.
